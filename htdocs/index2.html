Causes of Global Warming

The main causes of global warming are the emissions of greenhouse gases and deforestation. Greenhouse gas emissions come from a variety of sources, such as the burning of fossil fuels for energy, the usage of fertilizers and other chemicals in agriculture that contain greenhouse gases, and the increase of livestock farming which contributes heavily to the emission of methane, a greenhouse gas.

1. Power and Energy Generation
In generating electricity for homes, factories, etc., efficient energy sources must be used. Currently, the three fossil fuels: coal, oil, and natural gas are among the most common sources of energy, therefore, they are the most used sources of energy by people, currently. Burning these fossil fuels to create energy releases a lot of greenhouse gases. The burning of gasoline to power cars also pollutes the air and releases Carbon Dioxide, a greenhouse gas. This is why switching to renewable energy sources is a major aspect of fighting global warming and climate change.
2. Harmful Farming Practices
In agriculture, farmers need to grow crops as quickly as possible. They also need to optimize the size and health of the crops. Therefore, some farmers use pesticides and fertilizers that contain greenhouse gases. The farming of livestock also contributes a lot to greenhouse gas emissions. The waste products of some livestock animals like cows is one of the biggest contributors of Methane, which is an example of a greenhouse gas.
3. Deforestation
The causes of global warming don't only consist of the sources of greenhouse gases; they may also be from the absence of things that regulate greenhouse gas levels, like trees. Trees are cut down due to natural causes like wildfires, or because of human activities, like logging. Deforestation lessens the number of trees that help absorb and regulate greenhouse gases, specifically Carbon Dioxide, CO2. As we all know, trees absorb Carbon Dioxide and release Oxygen. When the trees are cut down, the Carbon Dioxide they were able to absorb is released back into the environment.
