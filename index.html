What is Global Warming?

Global warming is basically the gradual heating of the Earth, mainly due to the sun's heat being trapped in the Earth's atmosphere by greenhouse gasses, including Methane and Carbon Dioxide. The greenhouse gases trap the sun's heat, allowing them to enter the Earth but preventing them from exiting. This excess heat negatively affects and influences the climate of different places around the world, resulting in more natural disasters and the rise of the sea level. Recently, global warming has been at an all-time high, and the problem must be solved before it is too late.
